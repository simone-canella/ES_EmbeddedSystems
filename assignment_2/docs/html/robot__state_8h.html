enum ObAvPhase_t

Enumeration of sub-phases executed sequentially during obstacle avoidance.

Enumerator
OBAV_PHASE_STOP 

Halt current movement and zero velocities.

OBAV_PHASE_ROTATE_CW 

Perform initial 90-degree right turn.

OBAV_PHASE_MOVE_FW 

Drive straight to bypass the obstacle.

OBAV_PHASE_ROTATE_CCW 

Perform final 90-degree left turn to resume heading.

OBAV_PHASE_EXIT 

Evaluate environment and exit maneuver.
